About NyayaVaani

NyayaVaani is a revolutionary AI-powered legal assistant designed to make justice accessible to every Indian citizen, in their native language. We combine cutting-edge AI, voice recognition, and secure data handling to provide instant, reliable legal guidance — anytime, anywhere.

Built by two passionate students, this project bridges the gap between complex legal systems and everyday people.
